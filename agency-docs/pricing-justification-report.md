# Pricing Justification Report: The Mongolian Market

Based on current (2023-2024) market data for web development, e-commerce, and digital marketing in Mongolia, this report analyzes and justifies your 3-Tier pricing strategy.

## 1. Grounded Market Data (Mongolia)

We researched the standard rates for digital services in Mongolia. Here is the reality of the market your clients face when they look for alternatives:

### A. Website Development Costs
*   **Basic/Static Website (Танилцуулга хуудас):** ₮300,000 - ₮1,490,000. These are simple brochures with no transactional capabilities. 
*   **Corporate Dynamic Website (Байгууллагын вэбсайт):** ₮2,000,000 - ₮4,000,000+. Includes basic CMS, multiple pages, but no complex sales funnels.
*   **E-Commerce Website (Онлайн дэлгүүр):** ₮5,000,000 - ₮40,000,000+. Full shopping cart systems, payment gateway integrations (QPay, SocialPay), and user accounts.
*   **SaaS E-commerce Platforms:** Local platforms like Zochil charge ~₮55,000/month, and Shoppy/Market.shop take significant commissions.

### B. Hidden & Recurring Costs (The Client's Burden)
Standard web agencies hit clients with ongoing fees:
*   .MN Domain Name: ~₮165,000 / year
*   SSL Certificate: ₮100,000 - ₮500,000 / year
*   Web Hosting: ₮100,000+ / year

### C. Digital Marketing & Staffing Costs
*   Hiring a dedicated Social Media Marketing Manager costs **₮1,200,000 - ₮2,500,000+ per month** in Ulaanbaatar.
*   Agency retainers for ad management and content creation typically start at ₮1,500,000/month and scale up.

## 2. Analysis of Your 3-Tier Strategy

Your pricing is not only justifiable; it is positioned as an extreme value-add for wholesalers and importers. You are exploiting a massive gap in the market between "useless cheap static sites" and "overkill ₮5M e-commerce builds."

### Tier 1: The Anchor (₮350,000)
*   **Market Context:** Fits exactly at the bottom-end of the Mongolian market for a basic, useless static website (₮300k - ₮890k).
*   **Verdict:** **Perfectly Justified.** It establishes that you *could* build them a standard, dumb website for market rate, but it serves effectively as a psychological decoy. Nobody should buy this, but it proves your base rate is fair.

### Tier 2: The Sales Machine (₮500,000 - ₮750,000)
*   **Market Context:** A client who has dead stock and needs to sell online normally has two bad choices:
    1.  Pay a web agency **₮5,000,000+** for a full e-commerce site they don't need for a single product line.
    2.  Pay Zochil ₮55,000/month forever and still have to figure out how to build the page and funnel themselves.
*   **Your Value Prop:** You are giving them a frictionless, transactional solution (with automated Telegram routing and tracking pixels) for the price of a mid-tier static website. 
*   **Verdict:** **Massively Underpriced (In a Good Way).** This is a no-brainer for a wholesaler. You are delivering e-commerce-level utility (sales capture) without the e-commerce price tag (₮5M). By hosting on GitHub Pages/Netlify and using Telegram instead of complex backends, you eliminate their ongoing hosting/SSL costs, saving them another ₮300,000+ a year.

### Tier 3: The Agency Partner (₮1,500,000+)
*   **Market Context:** To get a sales page, 3 video reels, ad copy, and 2 weeks of ad management, a client would normally have to:
    1. Hire an agency to build the site (₮2M+)
    2. Hire a videographer for reels (₮500k+)
    3. Hire a performance marketer (₮1M+/mo)
*   **Verdict:** **Highly Competitive.** You are replacing a ₮3.5M+ upfront agency stack with a single ₮1.5M invoice. This tier is an absolute bargain for a business owner who has money but zero time or technical skill.

4.  **The ROI Angle:** "If you have ₮20,000,000 wrapped up in that container, a ₮750,000 investment to liquidate it is a 3.7% marketing cost. That is exceptionally cheap."

Your pricing strategy is airtight.
