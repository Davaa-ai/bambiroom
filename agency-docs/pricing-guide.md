# The Complete Guide to Pricing & Charging
*How to avoid undercharging and position yourself as a high-value partner.*

## 1. The Core Principle: Value-Based Pricing
**Never charge by the hour. Never charge for "a website."**
When you charge for a "website", clients compare you to the cheapest option (e.g., a kid on Fiverr or a basic Wix template). You lose that race to the bottom.

**You charge for the VALUE you create.**
Ask yourself: *What is the financial outcome of my work for this client?*
If an importer has ₮50,000,000 in dead stock, and your landing page helps them liquidate it, you didn't build a ₮100,000 website. You solved a ₮50,000,000 problem. Charging 10% (₮5,000,000) for a ₮50M solution is fair. Charging 1% (₮500,000) makes it an absolute steal for them.

*Always anchor your price against the money they will make (or save).*

---

## 2. The Tiered Pricing Structure (The "Decoy" Effect)
Never give a client just ONE price. If you give one price (e.g., ₮500,000), their only two options are "Yes" or "No." 
Instead, give them **Three Options**. This changes their psychology from "Should I buy this?" to *"Which one should I buy?"*

### Tier 1: The "Basic Setup" (The Anchor)
*   **Price:** ₮350,000
*   **What they get:** The bare minimum. Basic template, they provide all copy, no complex Telegram integration (just standard email forms), no Pixel setup. 
*   **Purpose:** This exists to make Tier 2 look like an incredible deal. You actually *don't* want them to buy this.

### Tier 2: The "Sales Machine" (The Goal)
*   **Price:** ₮500,000 to ₮750,000
*   **What they get:** The Taobao-style premium page, custom copywriting (pain/solution), Telegram Bot instant order routing, Meta Pixel tracking setup.
*   **Purpose:** This is what you actually want to sell. Because it’s compared to the basic tier, it feels like much better value.

### Tier 3: The "Agency Partner" (The Premium)
*   **Price:** ₮1,500,000+
*   **What they get:** Everything in Tier 2, PLUS you make 3 reel videos for them, write 5 Facebook ad scripts, and manage their Facebook ads manager for the first 2 weeks. 
*   **Purpose:** Some businesses just want you to handle everything. When someone eventually buys this, you just 3x'd your revenue for a few extra hours of work.

## 3. Psychological Tactics & Scripts (Mongolian Market)

### Tactic A: The "Reframing" Tactic
*Client:* "₮500,000 үнэтэй байна аа. Фэйсбүүк хуудас нээхэд үнэгүй шдээ." *(500k is expensive. Making a FB page is free.)*
*You:* "Тийм ээ, фэйсбүүк хуудас үнэгүй. Гэхдээ таны бараа яг одоо фэйсбүүк дээр үнэгүй зарагдаж чадаж байгаа бил үү? Би танд вебсайт биш, барааг тань автоматаар зардаг 'Худалдагч' хийж өгөх гэж байна. Нэг сайн худалдагчийг 1 сар ажиллуулахад 1.5 сая төгрөг цалин өгнө. Би танд 500 мянгаар хэзээ ч унтдаггүй, цалин нэхдэггүй дижитал худалдагч бүтээж өгөх гэж байна." 
*(Yes, but is your product selling smoothly there now? I'm not selling a website, I'm building you an automated 'Salesperson.' A real salesperson costs 1.5M/month. I'm building a digital one that never sleeps for 500k.)*

### Tactic B: The "Risk Reversal" (If they are terrified of losing money)
*Client:* "Зарагдахгүй бол яах вэ? Би 500к алдах уу?" *(What if it doesn't sell? Do I lose 500k?)*
*You:* "Бидний хийсэн хуудасруу хүн орсон л бол лавтай 10-20% нь авдаг. Хэрвээ та санаа зовж байгаа бол бид ₮250,000-аар эхлээд тохируулж өгье. Зарагдсан бараа болгоноос ₮5,000 авъя."
*(Offer the Hybrid model. Lower the upfront fee, take a small commission. This proves you have skin in the game).*

### Tactic C: The "Silence" Tactic
When you state your price: *"Энэхүү борлуулалтын системийг бүрэн угсрахад ₮750,000 болно."*
**STOP TALKING.**
Do not apologize. Do not immediately offer a discount. Silence makes the buyer process the value. The first person to speak loses negotiation leverage.

## 4. How to Ask for the Money (Upfront vs. Split)
*   **Never start work without money changing hands.** If a client won't pay *something* upfront, they are not serious and they will waste your time.
*   **Standard Terms:** 50% upfront to start work. 50% upon delivery and testing of the Telegram bot. 
*   **Script:** "Системийн хөгжүүлэлт 2 өдөр үргэлжилнэ. Ажил эхлэхэд 50% (₮250,000), бүрэн тест хийж дуусаад танд хүлээлгэж өгөхдөө үлдэгдэл 50%-г авах нөхцөлтэй."

## Summary Cheatsheet
1. **Never charge hourly.** Charge based on the problem you solve.
2. **Never offer just one price.** Use the 3-Tier system.
3. **Always take 50% upfront.** It filters out non-serious clients.
4. **Anchor your price** against the value of their trapped dead stock.
